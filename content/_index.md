artQ13 is not an exhibition space. It is a research device operating since 2014 in the gap between artistic practice and critical thought, between action and document.

We don't produce exhibitions: we activate processes. Each project begins with a question — not a calendar, not a format. Working with artists, composers, performers, and writers is the method, not the content.

Based in Rome, Viale della Piramide Cestia 1. Independent, non-commercial.
